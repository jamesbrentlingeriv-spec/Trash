# 🚨 STOP THE RUMPKE EXPANSION 🚨

## Montgomery County is NOT a Dumping Ground!

This is a site dedicated to fighting back against the proposed expansion of the Rumpke landfill in Montgomery County, Kentucky. **We will not be silenced. We will not be ignored. We will not let our community be destroyed for corporate profit.**

---

## THE PROBLEM

Rumpke has already proven it **CANNOT** manage the current landfill operations responsibly. Yet they want to expand? 

- **The stench is unbearable.** Residents can't open their windows. Families are forced to live in sealed homes.
- **Property values are PLUMMETING.** Our homes—our life savings—are becoming worthless.
- **Our health is at risk.** Leachate management failures, soil contamination threats, and chemical runoff threaten everything we hold dear.
- **Rumpke has BROKEN every promise they've made.** They promised odor control. They delivered failure.

**This is not acceptable. This is not negotiable.**

---

## THE REALITY

Rumpke has:
- ❌ Failed to implement promised odor control technology
- ❌ Accumulated major regulatory violations for leachate management
- ❌ Ignored community petitions (2,000+ signatures)
- ❌ Demonstrated complete disregard for our well-being

And now they want to **DOUBLE DOWN** by expanding operations? **ABSOLUTELY NOT.**
